About.

Neverday was formed in late 2018/early 2019 in Rimbo, Sweden.
The band consists of Emelie Hanqvist on vocals, Peter Almgren (ex. Sahira, Aurora, A-bros) on guitar and backing vocals, Kristoffer Heiskanen (ex. Sahira) on lead guitar, Joakim E. Sköldqvist on drums and Daniel Wejdin (Räfven, ex. Kultiration) on bass.

Neverday plays punk/rock with influences from metal, hardcore and skatepunk. Combining energy and catchy melodies with lyrics about love, death and anxiety.

Neverday's debut single "Gray" was released 25th of april 2020. It was recorded att legendary Sunlight Studio by producer Thomas Skogsberg, the mastermind behind records like Entombed - Left Hand Path and The Hellacopters - Payin´ The Dues.
